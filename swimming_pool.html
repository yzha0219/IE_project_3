Swimming Pool

Guideline

Supervision

• Make sure your children are always under your supervision

• Supervise children under 5 years old within arms' reach

• Don't let older children supervise younger children!

• Keep an eye on your children's pool party

• Do not perform multi tasks like playing with mobile phones

• Prepare everything e.g. towels, dry clothes, cordless phone or answering machine

Pool Fence

• Ensure the distance between the bottom rail and the ground is no more than 100mm

• Ensure the vertical gaps are no more than 100mm apart

• Make sure the height is at least 1.2 m

• Make sure the fence panels are in place and attached

• Make sure there are no holes on the fence

• Check if the screws are rusted , loose or missing

Pool toy safety

• Make sure the product suit your kids' age

• Deflate and store the pool toy after use

• Inflatable toys are checked before use for any wear and tear

• Pool toys are used for recommended purpose only

• Read the instructions before use

• Remove the toys from the pool after use

Around the pool

• Remove climbable or dangerous objects (e.g. chairs, bikes, ladders, trees, pot plants, BBQs)

• Remove pool chemicals

• Trim trees and shrubs near the pool and pool fence

• Keep pool away from electricity

Preparation

• Make sure your kids have enrolled in swimming lessons before they go swimming

• Make sure your have enrolled in first aid courses and your CPR and first aid skills are up to date

• Prepare a First Aid Kit handy

• Buy and install Resuscitation Sign in the private pool area/p>
