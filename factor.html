Did You Know?

• It only takes 20 SECONDS and a FEW CENTIMETERS of the water for children to DROWN

• Close and constant Adult SUPERVISION at all times is a MUST

• Swimming pools, Lakes, beaches, Dams and water tanks are all WATER HAZARDS

• Learn FIRST AID and CPR in case of an EMERGENCY situation

WHO DROWNS? (Children Age 0-14)

• Average of 38 Children were dead from drowning past 10 years

• Rate of drowning deaths increases every year

• Children of age between 0 to 14 years recorded highest water falities rate

WHEN DO DROWNINGS OCCUR?

• The highest number of drowning deaths occured in Summer with 42% (113) in 2017

• followed by on the weekends around afternoon (12:00pm-6:00pm)

WHERE DO DROWNING OCCUR?

• Highest rate of the place where drowning deaths occured is the Beach(35%)

• Swimming pools, Lakes, beaches, Dams and water tanks are all water hazards

• Parents need to be well-informed and educated with safety rules and first aid

• Make sure children are under adult provision at all times

LEARN HOW TO SWIM!

• It is important to learn how to swim and survive

• Swimming is the highest sport activities that children participate in Australia

• Children should be aware of water safety and hazards
