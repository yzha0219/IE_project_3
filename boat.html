Guideline

Boat and Ship

DO NOT DRINK

• Drinking alcohol may cause accidents such as falling and drownings from the boat

• Boat driver should not be drinking alcohol when driving

CHECK the WEATHER and WATER CONDITION

• Avoid bad weather condition while out for water activities

• Make sure to check weather forecst before and during activities

WEAR FLOTATION DEVICE

• Ensure all people on board wear LIFE JACKETS

• Need to be properly fitted to be used effectively

LET PEOPLE KNOW

• Always let people know WHERE you are going and WHEN you will be back

• Send photos of a boat you are riding to people around you

• FULLY charge your phone battery

REMEMBER to get HELP

• CALL 000 on the phone

• Marine Radio

• VHF : Ch 16 or VHF :Distress button

• Activate flare when you are in danger and see any rescuers
